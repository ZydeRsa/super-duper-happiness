Top Places to Visit

  • Lake Fundudzi - A sacred lake surrounded by legends and stunning scenery.
  • Thathe Vondo Forest - Ancient forest rich in biodiversity and cultural heritage.
  • Phiphidi Falls - Beautiful cascading waterfalls perfect for nature lovers.
  • Dzata Ruins and Museum of the Drum - Historical ruins and cultural museum showcasing Venda drums.
  • Thulamela - Archaeological site with ancient ruins offering a glimpse into Venda's past.
  • Kokwane Prehistoric Footprints - Fossilized footprints of prehistoric creatures and early humans.
  • Lwamondo Hill - A scenic hill with panoramic views and hiking trails.
  • Mphaphuli Cycad Reserve - A reserve protecting rare cycads native to the region.
  • Guvhukuvhu Pool - A natural pool ideal for swimming and relaxing.
  • Tshatshingo Potholes - Natural rock formations and potholes created by river erosion.
